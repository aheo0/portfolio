Hello, my name is Apollo Heo, and I am currently enrolled to Dublin High School as a junior with aspiring hopes and goals for the next 2 years I will be spending in high school. Ever since I was young, I have always enjoyed technology in the ever-changing world we now live in, and have wanted to leave my own legacy on the internet. In my free time, I program various tasks that automate simple repetitive tasks.

I have done VEX Robotics for two years and have qualified for Worlds (the second year's was cancelled due to COVID). I have also mentored a middle school VEX team (2416X) where I applied the skills I gained from my team to teach younger kids and expose them to engineering skills typically not covered in school cirriculums, while also preparing them for their competitions. I also aimed to expose younger kids to STEM through Valley's Children Museum, a local non-profit organization that weekly (and twice a week over the summer) provides stations where the kids can create various projects, perform scientific experiments, or watch demonstrations that further enhance their scientific knowledge while simultaneously adding interest in STEM fields.

In my free time, I like to build programming projects. Some examples in the past have been a blog template, using SQL database and with custom back-end and front-end support, a Discord bot, made using its API, that controls up to 10,000 people's voice permissions at a time, and a logistic model that attempted to predict the spread of COVID when it first came out using data web-scraped from online government databases.

Beside these programming projects, I also study algorithms through the United States Computing Olympiad (USACO) and mathematics beyond those taught in school through the American Mathematics Competition (AMC).
